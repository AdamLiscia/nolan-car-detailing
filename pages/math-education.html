My Teaching Journey

My passion for mathematics education began at Westchester Community College's Math Lab, where I discovered my love for helping others understand and appreciate mathematics. This experience sparked a lifelong commitment to breaking down mathematical barriers and fostering confidence in students.

After teaching in The Bronx for two years and exploring data science, I found my true calling in one-on-one tutoring through the School for Young Performers (SYP). This transition allowed me to develop personalized approaches to mathematics education that cater to each student's unique needs.
